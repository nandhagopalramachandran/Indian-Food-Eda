About The Project

Indian cuisine consists of a variety of regional and traditional cuisines native to the Indian subcontinent.

Given the diversity in soil, climate, culture, ethnic groups, and occupations, these cuisines vary substantially and use locally available spices, herbs, vegetables, and fruits. Indian food is also heavily influenced by cultural choices and traditions.

Indian Food EDA is an innovative project that explores the rich and diverse world of Indian cuisine through comprehensive data analysis and insightful visualizations.

Our interface offers an interactive dashboard, detailed reports, and engaging narratives that showcase the cultural heritage and culinary traditions of India.

With a user-friendly web interface, we aim to provide valuable insights and resources for food enthusiasts, researchers, and professionals passionate about Indian gastronomy.
